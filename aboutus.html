History of Indarti Silver

All of our products are made out of 925 Sterling Silver, which contains 92.5% silver and 7.5% other metals.

Since 1986, Indarti Silver, has provided fine .925 Sterling Silver Jewelry to retailers and wholesalers across the world. Based in Celuk Village, Gianyar regency, Bali, Indonesia, Indarti Silver is known for its fashionable and affordable handcrafted Sterling Silver jewelry with various designs. Our jewelry are made by the finest silversmiths.

Our fashionable and unique Sterling silver jewelry will accessorize all budgets and lifestyles. Our large selection on earrings, earstuds, bracelets, rings, and necklaces, come with variety of semi-precious stones (Amethyst, Topaz, Peridot, Garnet, Citrine, larimar, opal, amazonite, lapiz,lazuli, turquoise, rainbow moonstone, labradorite), shells (paua/abalone, mother of pearl, siwa eye shell), Fresh water pearl, Red Coral, etc.

We provide competitive wholesale prices for wholesale buyer. You can rely on Indarti Silver as an innovative and reliable partner to establish or grow your business with. We will look forward to being your wholesale source for Sterling Silver Jewelry.
